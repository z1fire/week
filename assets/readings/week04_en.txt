Today we go to school together.
The teacher is introducing a new student.
I ask him: What is your name?
He answers: I am a Chinese student.
He really likes exercise and also often runs.
I like drinking coffee the most, and he likes drinking tea the most.
Outside the school, there is a shop.
In the shop, there is fruit, eggs, and milk.
I buy an apple, and he buys a watermelon.
We are all very happy. Goodbye!
