Today we go to the park and look at many animals and plants.
In the park there are birds, fish, monkeys, and also pandas.
Pandas are very quiet, and monkeys are very lively.
Tigers and snakes make people afraid, but I think they are also very interesting.
The teacher says that Earth’s environment is very important, and pollution can affect animals and plants.

We also discuss changes in climate, temperature, and weather.
Maybe the problem is big, but as long as we work hard together, there is hope.
In short, whether in the city or the countryside, the environment needs people’s care.
